What is CustomFireTab?

CustomFireTab is a simple yet powerful Firefox extension that gives you full control over the URL of your new tabs.

Whether it's a specific search engine, a personal dashboard, or any favorite website, CustomFireTab allows you to customize every new tab according to your preference.

Key Features

  • Set any URL as the default page for new tabs.
  • Easy setup through the extension settings.
  • Quickly change your custom URL.
  • Privacy-friendly, with settings stored locally on your device.

How to Install CustomFireTab

  1. Visit the Firefox Add-ons Store and find CustomFireTab.
  2. Click "Add to Firefox" to install the extension.
  3. Open the extension settings and enter your custom URL, then save!
